Equipment we are using

To clean your carpets, we are using the Airflex Storm carpet cleaning machine. This is one of the most powerful carpet cleaning machines on the market fitted with a powerful 600 psi pump. The machine is also built with 2 x powerful 1200 wat Lamb Ametek standard 3-stage vac motors for extracting the dirt and soil of your carpets.
