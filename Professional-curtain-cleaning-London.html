Professional Curtain Cleaning London

The perfect set of curtains can transform the look of a room in one easy step but only if they are clean and pressed without any traces of dirt or stains. Besides, cleaning the curtains at home might be a difficult task to accomplish.
In most cases, you don't really need to take down your heavy curtains and haul them to the cleaners.

My Magic Cleaners helps you to add a fresh, clean look to your home with cleaner curtains.We clean all types of curtains, no matter how heavy, delicate or expensive they might be including flat panel curtains, table top curtains, grommet curtains, sash curtains, rod pocket curtains, drapes.

Clean and nice looking curtains in 6 steps:
· Pre - testing is carried out to establish the nature of the fabric.
· Carefully selecting detergents in order to preserve the true colour.
· Our steam cleaning team uses chemicals that are non-toxic and eco-friendly.
· Stains are pretreated using special stain removal agents appropriate to the type of stain.
· Our team uses the power of steam to extract the dirt, stain and pollutants stuck on your curtains.
· Deodorizing is the final processes of our curtain cleaning service.
